Welcome to ReserveMe.

ReserveMe is a platform to reserve spaces at the University, Student Organisations, Non Profits affliated with the University etc are able to reserve spaces at the Student Union. ReserveMe is also used for creating events at the University which cater to the student community, if you are a Student Org and you plan to do events on campus where you also need reservation. Then ReserveMe is your One way stop.
